Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking

What documents do I need?

For self-drive — a valid Driving License and Aadhaar Card. For chauffeur-driven — just your booking confirmation. All documents verified digitally.

Is there a security deposit?

Yes, a refundable deposit of ₹2,000–₹5,000 depending on the car category. Returned in full within 24–48 hours after you return the car.

Is fuel included in the rental price?

Self-drive: Fuel is on you (same-to-same fuel policy). Chauffeur-driven outstation: Fuel is included in the package price.

Can I take the car outstation?

Absolutely! We offer packages to Lonavala, Alibaug, Mahabaleshwar, Pune, Goa, Shirdi, Nashik, and more. Both self-drive and chauffeur-driven available.

What if the car breaks down?

We provide 24/7 roadside assistance. Call our helpline and we'll send a mechanic or replacement car, free of charge.

What's your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 6 hours before pickup. After that, 50% charge applies. No-shows are charged in full.

Are the cars insured?

Yes, every single vehicle carries comprehensive insurance coverage. You're fully protected.

What payment methods do you accept?

UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Debit Cards, Credit Cards, Net Banking, and Cash. Corporate clients get monthly invoicing with GST.
